Days of isolation

Working from home required

March 24, 2020 Joan Tolos

8 minute read

As the world is facing the pandemic caused by this new Coronavirus strain, most of my colleagues and myself are forced to work from home. I want to share my experience working without leaving the house. Particular circumstances I want to point out that this is not just “working from home”, these are extreme circumstances. In my case, the most defining one is the fact that the schools are closed. That means that my two kids (which are four and two) have to be with my wife and myself at home and due to their ages, we can not leave them alone.
